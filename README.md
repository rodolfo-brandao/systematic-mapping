# Mining Depression Signs on Social Media: A Systematic Mapping

## About
This repository contains the final paper, of my authorship, on the execution of a systematic mapping of the literature regarding academic trends in the Computer Science field, related to the extraction of information from social networks in order to identify possible cases of depression among its users.

## Structure
The artice has 6 main sections:
* Introduction
* Related Works
* Methodology
* Analysis of Results
* Threats to Validity
* Conclusion

## Availability
You can find my research available in this repository in `PDF` and `TeX` formats. Feel free to choose one of your preference to analyze this work.

<br>

> Although the reference articles analyzed in the systematic mapping were found in english, the article presented here was written in pt-BR given its academic needs.
